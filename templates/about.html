About HouseMate

HouseMate is a household chore tracker.

It is a fun and easy way for housemates or families to organise and share household tasks.

You can add chores to the to-do list for everyone to see, see who has requested each chore, and keep track of who has completed them.

With the leaderboard, it makes a game of managing chores making it more fun for everyone!
